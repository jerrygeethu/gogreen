Go green with Farming
GO GREEN...The first step we take in accomplishing our dream of making the world a greener place to live in. It is a whole new idea of generating prosperity through taking up agriculture in its right perspective.
The design and specifications of every farm-plot keeps an eye on the future while taking into consideration the needs of past. Each of the 10-acre plots boasts of fertile soil that would offer any cultivator high yields in crops.

Specialty of plot

Out of 300 acres of cultivated land area, each 10-acre has bio fencing with Choukka, 500 numbers of Teak plants and a mixture of high yielding varieties of Mango, Supporta, Coconut and Gooseberry. National Horticultural Board and Tamil Nadu Agricultural University guide the project with long term experience and expertise in farming.

More Facilities

Each 10-acre plot has additional facilities. Independent road access, around 500 square feet farm house, electrical connection, telephone connection, 100% drip irrigation and supervision by experienced staff are a few of the highlights.

The premises of Go Green farms offer a swimming pool where one can enjoy swimming and refreshing bath, kids playground for kids to spend quality time, recreational facilities and an amusement park to keep one entertained by night and day, an organic kitchen garden and horticultural garden from where first lessons of cultivation can be picked up, a vermi compost pit and a small dairy farm. Guests can avail the 5 bed room guest house facility as well.

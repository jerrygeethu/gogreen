Tourism  
Prime Move’s travel venture Elatrip.com sees Farm Tourism as a focus area. And we plan to utilise the Theni Farms as a tourism destination.  Travellers will be invited to visit our farms to learn the finer elements of plantation and enjoy the serene surroundings.

For this purpose, a guest house with five bedrooms is being developed. It has other facilities like swimming pool, playground, amusement park, organic kitchen garden, dairy farm, vermin-compost plant and horticulture garden.

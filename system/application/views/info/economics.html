Cost per plot is Rs. 30 lakh. This covers all costs like land value, planting, manuring and maintenance for the first three years. After three years, a service charge will be applicable for the maintenance of the farm. At present, it is fixed as 20% of the net income from the plot. The buyers will also have the option to run the farm of their own after the initial three year period.

The booking amount is 10% of the total coast. The first installment to be given at the time of registration is 40% of the total amount. The remaining 50% is to be given in installments in the next 24 months. (Details Here)

Welcome  
Farming is as old a profession as human civilization. Over the centuries, its philosophy has changed, evolving along with Man’s march ahead. From being a means for survival and livelihood, farming has now become an avenue for secure investment.   

When we Go Green, it’s a dream come true. The dream of turning the red of the Earth into the green of vegetation.

Prime Move enters large scale farming with a whole new approach. Surely, we are here to generate wealth. But profit is not the sole goal for us. Ours is a long term approach, caring the land, water, vegetation, people and revenue.

We will be integrating the farms with our tourism operations as well. Our travel venture elatrip.com will invite tourists to our farms, in the coming months.

Theni Farms is a realization of our vision, our experience.  Equipped with the new age know- how and traditional expertise, we move ahead.
